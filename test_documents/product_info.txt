Product Information - AI Assistant Platform

What is our platform?
Our platform is an intelligent AI-powered customer support assistant that works across multiple messaging channels including WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, and Facebook Messenger. It helps businesses automate customer conversations, capture leads, and provide 24/7 support.

Key Features:
- Multi-channel support (WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, Messenger)
- GPT-4 powered intelligent responses
- Knowledge base with RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
- Conversation memory for context-aware responses
- Custom AI rules for business-specific behavior
- Lead capture and CRM integration
- Human handoff when needed
- Real-time analytics and insights
- Multi-language support

How does it work?
1. Customers message your business on any connected channel
2. Our AI analyzes the message and retrieves relevant knowledge
3. AI generates an intelligent, context-aware response
4. If needed, the conversation is handed off to a human agent
5. All conversations are logged and analyzed for insights

Benefits:
- Reduce response time from hours to seconds
- Handle unlimited conversations simultaneously
- Never miss a lead or customer inquiry
- Provide consistent, accurate information 24/7
- Free up your team to focus on complex issues
- Scale your customer support without hiring more staff

Who is it for?
- Small businesses looking to improve customer service
- E-commerce stores handling product inquiries
- Service businesses managing appointments and bookings
- Real estate agents responding to property inquiries
- Any business that receives customer messages regularly

Setup time:
Getting started takes only 15-20 minutes. No technical expertise required.
